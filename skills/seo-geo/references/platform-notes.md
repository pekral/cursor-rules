# Platform-Specific Optimization Notes

## ChatGPT / OpenAI

- Brand recognition and freshness matter for citation
- Backlinks and clear structure support citation likelihood
- Allow `ChatGPT-User` and `GPTBot` in `robots.txt` for search features

## Perplexity

- Allow Perplexity-related crawling per `robots.txt` policy (`Perplexity-User`)
- FAQ schema and semantically tight copy help citation
- PDFs may be cited where applicable — ensure important PDFs are accessible

## Google (including AI Overviews)

- E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is central
- Structured data drives rich results and AI Overview inclusion
- Topical clusters and strong internal linking improve authority
- Citations and sourced claims support E-E-A-T signals

## Bing / Copilot

- Bing index coverage is prerequisite — verify pages are indexed
- Fast page load and mobile usability are ranking factors
- Clear entity definitions help Copilot extraction

## Claude (via search partners)

- Factual density and clear structure aid extraction
- Well-organized headings and concise paragraphs improve citation
- Allow `ClaudeBot` / `anthropic-ai` in `robots.txt`
